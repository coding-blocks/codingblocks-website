NEW STUDY CENTER IN NOIDA
Team Coding Blocks takes great pride in announcing that we are coming up with a brand new study centre, this time in Noida. Over the years, we got innumerable requests and complains regarding travel times from Noida, Ghaziabad and even East Delhi, so we thought it's about time we open a center in Noida.
 
The Noida center is located in Sector 2, at a distance of less than 50m from the Noida Sector-15 Metro Station. Connectivity through metro and other public transport is top notch, and the area is one of Noida's corporate hubs with offices like HCL, Aegis, IOCL, PDIL in the vicinity, thus ensuring maximum security.
 
Course registrations have already started and some batches have already filled. Check out all relevant details and links on the Noida Centre Page on our website. You can find the directions to our centre here. Looking forward to having you over at our new centre.

BOUNTIFUL OPEN SOURCE SUMMER (BOSS 2018) KICKS OFF
At Coding Blocks, a majority of our team members comes from a vibrant Open Source Community, and that makes us strongly believe in free and open source software. Last year, we launched Bountiful Open Source Summer (BOSS), to stay in touch with our alumni, and to imbibe the feeling of open source development in everyone. And we're back!  
 
This year, anyone who's a bona fide student in India as of May 15, 2018 is eligible for participation in BOSS. You can contribute to any project part of the Coding Blocks Github Repo, and earn bounties if accepted. At the end of the competition, the top 5 bounty scoring students will win a Macbook, a One Plus 6 smartphone, a PS4 Slim console, a Fitbit Charge 2 band and an Amazon Kindle respectively. All top 10 scorers will also receive specially curated Coding Blocks goodie bags. So what are you waiting for? Let's get going!

CODING BLOCKS @ GOOGLE I/O 2018
This May, one of Coding Blocks' Android mentors, Harshit Dwivedi, was invited over to San Francisco by Google, to witness Google I/O 2018, LIVE! As a multpile time GSoC Mentor and an Associate Android Developer, Harshit has been consistently working closely on all aspects of Android.
