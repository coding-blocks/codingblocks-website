KNOWLEDGE BLOCKS
To promote the spirit of knowledge, we conducted an online quiz series on the Coding Blocks Facebook page. Knowledge Blocks was a quiz series based on all things tech and comprised ten questions asked over a ten-day period. With speed and accuracy being important factors, we saw a participation of more than a thousand over the ten-day period. At the end of the series, the top three participants giving the most number of answers in the fastest time were selected for exciting prizes.
CODERS' MEET AT NSIT
Coding Blocks conducted a one of its kind interaction session for enthusiastic coders, Coders' After Exam Meet-up @ McCain, NSIT on November 25. This was an interactive event wherein young coders from NSIT had discussions with CB mentors as well as senior NSITians about their doubts on academics, placements, societies and a lot more. The session helped young students get to know their seniors, and clarify their doubts, thus resulting in an enjoyable afternoon.
WEBINARS AND WORKSHOPS
OPEN SOURCE-101 @
GREATER NOIDA
Coding Blocks conducted a free workshop on the Basics of Open Source Software at its Greater Noida centre on the weekend of November 18-19. The workshop, conducted by GSoC mentor Anuj Garg, covered the basic concepts of open sourcing, the ideas and motives behind it as well as important topics such as Git, GitHub and Gitter. The workshop also covered suitable examples and implementations, as well as GSoC guidance.
MACHINE LEARNING
WEBINAR
One of our founding members, Prateek Narang conducted a webinar on Hands-on Machine Learning on November 6. The webinar was conducted on our YouTube channel and covered topics such as Python for Machine Learning, KNN algorithms and hand-written character recognition project on MNIST data-set. The webinar was attended by over 200 students live. The code is available here and the video can be seen here.

ARNAV GUPTA SPEAKS
AT GDG DEVFEST - 
GORKY
One of our founding members, Arnav Gupta, presented a talk at the GDG Dev Fest in Gorky, Russia on Developing Apps for Android Auto. The talk covered topics such as the downloading of Android Auto APIs, setting up the Android Auto Simulator, Deploying apps to the Auto Simulator as well as Publishing Android Auto apps. The workshop also covered building UIs suited for the Android Auto interface.
HIRING EVENT @
GREATER NOIDA
Coding Blocks organized a one of its kind hiring event for 2016 and 2017 graduates at its Greater Noida centre. The event saw multiple companies come in to hire students with good coding skills for the industry. The selection procedure included coding questions as well as general interviews. Many companies visited the event, one of which was LB Technologies, Gurgaon. It was an extremely useful event both for the companies as well as the students.
